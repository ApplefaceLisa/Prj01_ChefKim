Serving the Bay Area since 1980

Our restaurant is centrally located in San Francisco, California allowing us to service San Francisco, San Mateo, Oakland, Berkeley, Fremont, Newark, Mountain View and the surrounding areas.

Since 1980, Chef Kim's restaurant has been serving San Francisco Bay Area for more than 30 years and we are one of the most popular restaurants of this area.
